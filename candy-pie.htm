candy pie: a 3d pie chart

based upon babylon.js

1st candy pie

A pie chart with 5 pies, variable height and a variable arc (pie 4 is smaller, applepie is larger then the others).

The background color is taken from the title above.

an other candy pie

This pie chart has also 5 pies, but all with same width (arc), but the pies are made higher then in the previous pie chart. This is done via the verticalFactor configuration option.

Pie chart number 3

This pie chart has 4 equal pieces and is shown in a canvas with an aspect ratio of 1 on 1. Some label-configuration options are used to position & color the labels on the pie.
